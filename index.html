Tokyo Awaits!

Visit the Beautiful City of Tokyo!As Japan's largest city, Tokyo is a cultural phenomenon. With it's bustling streets and beautiful temples and parks there is something to do for everyone.Come and visit this beautiful slice of the world today!

Things to Do

Sensoji Temple

The Sensoji Temple in Tokyo Asakusa district is one of the most colorful and most popular temple in japan. It is also Tokyo's oldest temple. The legend goes that in 628, two brothers found a statue of Kannon, the godddess of mercy, in the Sumida River. When the brothers tried to return the statue it always found its way back to them. Thus, the temple was built in her honor. This temple really shows the rich culture that tokyo has to offer.

Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden

This beautiful park is the Shinjuku National Park. Located just west of downtown Tokyo, this garden houses multiple diffrent landscaping styles and if you go at the right time of year, you will see the breathtaking cherry blossoms come to bloom. Stroll through, have a picnic and take in this breathtaking garden.
